    Bella Vista Resort Hurghada.

    Bella Vista Hotel & Resort is located in the middle of the downtown of Sakkala in Hurghada City on the magnificent shores of the Red Sea. Bella Vista Hurghada combines convenience and charm for a dream vacation or an unforgettable relaxing trip. The resort is 10 minutes from Hurghada International Airport and a 60-minute flight from Cairo, by car 500 km from Cairo and 270 km from Luxor. This unique hotel offers a Panoramic view of Red Sea, private sandy beach for the world's best scuba diving in the Red Sea's legendary waters, and endless activities from fitness to relaxation and water sports to desert excursions and night life. Discover the difference. The resort's 270 unique guest rooms, family rooms, accessible rooms and suites, each feature a private balcony or patio, all rooms are equipped with private bath and showers, hairdryer, individual air condition control, satellite LED, mini bar, direct international/local telephone. All rooms are provided with the latest fire alarm and smoke detectors. There are several options for dinning & entertainment at the resort's bars and restaurants and guests may try the best sea sight bar all over Hurghada in the Caribbean Bar. Whether active or relaxing, the Bella Vista Resort Hurghada has appeal for every traveler.
